Project #5: Dual Lighting

Dual Lighting

Dual light effect in photoshop has the subject in the center with 2 color lighting, one color on left and other on the right side. The best use of dual lighting could be easily found in cinematography. You might have noticed, how the lighting on characters change in movies according to the emotion required in the scene.
Elements to avoid when creating the dual lighting effect in PhotoShop. Images should not be edited already, images with subject looking away from camera are best, and also try to avoid full body images
